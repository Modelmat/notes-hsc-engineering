Aeronautical Engineering
========================

    One or more examples of aeronautical engineering must be used to develop an
    understanding of the scope and nature of this profession. Some examples
    include: design and construction of recreational aircraft, general aviation
    aircraft, military aircraft, space craft, agricultural aircraft,
    helicopters and home-built aircraft.
